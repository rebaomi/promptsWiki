# movie-posters PROMPT-TEMPLATES

这个文件只放电影海报与收藏海报骨架模板。

## 主海报模板

```text
Create a theatrical movie poster for [genre/story]. [main subject] in [setting], vertical composition, dramatic lighting, [palette], cinematic atmosphere, strong focal point, elegant negative space for title, premium key art quality
```

```text
生成一张院线电影主海报：类型是[题材/故事]，主体为[主角/核心画面]，场景是[环境]，竖版构图，戏剧化光线，[配色]，整体具有电影感和强主视觉，预留片名空间，像正式上映海报。
```

## 角色海报模板

```text
Create a character poster for [character], [pose/expression], [environment/background], [light], vertical framing, restrained but intense mood, premium official poster finish
```

## 双角色海报模板

```text
Generate a dual-character poster for [Character A] and [Character B], [back-to-back or confrontation composition], half-body to upper thigh framing, faction contrast, strong visual tension, premium collector-poster finish
```

## 群像海报模板

```text
Create an ensemble epic poster based on [cast], central lead in front, supporting characters arranged in hierarchy around them, worldbuilding in the background, monumental cinematic composition, strong title space
```

## 极简收藏卡模板

```text
Generate a minimal collector-card-style poster for [character/theme], single dominant subject, large negative space, very few symbolic supporting elements, refined typography area, premium print-like finish
```

## 排版预留模板

```text
Create a poster composition designed for typography: clean tagline space at the top, large title space in the lower third, narrow billing-block area at the bottom, avoid complex image detail in text zones
```
