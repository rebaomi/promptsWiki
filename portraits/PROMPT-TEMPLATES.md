# portraits PROMPT-TEMPLATES

这个文件只放人物写真与肖像类 prompt 骨架模板。

## 杂志肖像模板

```text
Generate an editorial portrait of [person description], wearing [outfit], in [background/location], [framing], [lighting], [lens], refined skin texture, elegant palette, luxury magazine quality, natural hands, no clutter
```

```text
生成一张杂志感肖像：主体是[人物描述]，穿着[服装]，位于[背景/环境]中，采用[构图]，[光线]，[镜头感]，肤质真实，色调高级克制，像高端杂志大片，手部自然，避免杂乱。
```

## 生活方式写真模板

```text
Create a natural lifestyle portrait of [person] in [daily-life scene], [natural light condition], candid pose, realistic skin and fabric texture, calm mood, no clutter, no over-retouching
```

## 职业人像模板

```text
Generate a professional portrait of [role/person] in [workplace], soft clean lighting, approachable but confident expression, realistic posture, polished corporate/editorial quality, no text, no clutter
```

## 情侣写真模板

```text
Create a couple portrait of [character A] and [character B] in [scene], [time of day/light], natural body language, coordinated palette, cinematic lifestyle photography, no crowd, no stiff posing
```

## 电影感人像模板

```text
Generate a cinematic portrait of [person], in [location], [cold/warm/desaturated palette], [lighting], subtle atmosphere, shallow depth of field, strong emotional restraint, film-still quality
```
